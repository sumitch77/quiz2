§ 03

Fair play & score monitoring

Quiz scores mean something, so we monitor gameplay activity to keep the leaderboard honest. This includes watching for patterns consistent with cheating — such as automated answering, exploiting bugs, or using outside help to gain an unfair advantage.

Confirmed cheating is enforced on an escalating schedule:

1st offense — 24-hour ban 2nd offense — 7-day ban 3rd+ offense — permanent ban

Any scores gained through cheating may also be removed from leaderboards, regardless of when the violation is discovered.

§ 04

Respectful conduct

Quiz is a space for everyone to enjoy. Abusive behavior, harassment, or irregular and offensive language directed at other users is not allowed, in usernames, messages, comments, or anywhere else on the platform.

Conduct violations are treated with the same escalating ban schedule described above — starting with a 24-hour ban and increasing in severity for repeat offenses, up to a permanent ban.

§ 05

Creating your own quizzes

You can build and publish your own quizzes for others to play — just keep them clean. Public quizzes must not contain:

  • Abusive, hateful, or racist content
  • Political propaganda or content intended to incite political conflict
  • Any other content designed to harass, demean, or target individuals or groups

Quizzes that violate this policy will be removed, and accounts responsible may face suspension or a permanent ban depending on the severity and frequency of the violation.

§ 06

Fraud, scams & deceptive links

Using Quiz to defraud or deceive other users — in any form — is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, sharing:

  • Fake or malicious links

  • Unauthorized loan apps or predatory lending schemes
  • Any other content designed to scam, phish, or financially harm another user
Zero tolerance: any confirmed attempt to defraud another user results in an immediate permanent ban — no escalation period.
