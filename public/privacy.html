§ 01

Information we collect

We only collect what's needed to run Quiz and make it better for you. That includes:

  • Account details — your name, email address, and password (stored encrypted, never in plain text).
  • Quiz activity — the quizzes you take, your scores, and progress, so you can track your own results over time.
  • Device & usage data — basic technical information like browser type and general location (city-level), used only to keep the site running smoothly.
  • Anything you choose to share — such as a profile picture or feedback you send us.

We do not collect sensitive information like payment card numbers directly — those are handled by trusted, PCI-compliant payment processors if you ever purchase something on Quiz.

§ 02

How we use it

Your data exists to make Quiz work better for you, never to work against you. Specifically, we use it to:

  • To personalise your experience!
  • Run your account, save your scores, and show your quiz history.
  • Recommend quizzes and topics you might enjoy.
  • Fix bugs, prevent abuse, and keep the platform secure.
  • Send you important updates — like security notices or changes to this policy.
§ 03

Storage & security

We take security seriously and treat it as a first-class feature, not an afterthought.

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Passwords are hashed — we never store or see your actual password.
  • Access to user data internally is limited to the few team members who need it to keep Quiz running.
  • We run regular security reviews and monitor for unusual activity.
§ 04

Sharing & third parties

We only share limited data with:

  • Service providers who help us host the site, send emails, or process payments — bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities, only if required by law, and only to the extent legally necessary.
  • You — if you choose to make your quiz results or profile public within the app.
§ 05

Cookies & analytics

Quiz uses a small number of cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We also use privacy-friendly analytics to understand which quizzes are popular and where the site can improve — this data is aggregated and not tied to your identity for marketing purposes.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

§ 06

Your rights & choices

It's your data, so you're always in control. You can:

  • Access or download a copy of your data at any time.
  • Correct or update your account information.
  • Delete your account and associated data permanently.
  • Opt out of non-essential emails whenever you like.

To exercise any of these, just reach out — see the contact section below.
